# Engineering Principles

- Preserve evidence back to LAN-folder or inbox source references.
- Keep AI-generated notes at `AI Organized` until human or SME action changes status.
- Use Markdown for wiki/reports and JSON for metadata/dictionaries/indexes.
- Avoid target architecture commitments unless they are explicitly reviewed in the right artifact.
