# Role Responsibility

## AI Assistant

- Prepare candidate interpretations from available evidence.
- Generate SME Review Packs in Markdown.
- Show confidence and evidence for each recommendation.
- Avoid inventing evidence.
- Avoid asking SMEs to validate target architecture or implementation design.

## SME

- Validate legacy program, file, field, job, report, screen, and batch-flow meaning.
- Select A/B/C/D/E answers where possible.
- Provide corrections when the options are incomplete or inaccurate.
- Add comments for ambiguity, missing context, or known exceptions.

## Engineering Team

- Maintain repository standards.
- Apply reviewed results into dictionaries and mapping candidates.
- Resolve conflicts and open questions.
- Keep LAN-folder references traceable.
